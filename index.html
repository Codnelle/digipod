FAQs

How do I submit designs requests?

You can mail us your requests and our team will handle it from there

How fast will I receive my designs?

You can set your own deadlines for designs but on average, we deliver a design within a couple of days.

I still have hours left this month, what do I do with them?

After each completed design, your Asana board will be updated with what work has been done and how long it took.

Can other people from the company submit design requests?

Yes, you can add as many team members as you like to your design board.

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time designer or a developer or a marketer?

A full-time designer/developer/marketer can be expensive and hard to source. Your company may not have enough work to keep a full-timer busy. Besides that, not every designer/developer has all the specialties you need. At DigiPod, you’ll have access to our whole team of designers, developers and marketers. That team includes graphic designers, web designers, App developers, Marketers, growth strategist – all for less than the price of a full-time designer.

Can I order a one-time service, like a logo?

We offer a combined All-In-One plan to help you with all your design needs. You’re welcome to upgrade for one month to get the designs you need, then return to your previous plan for the following month.

What programs do you use for design?

We work in a wide range of design software, including Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Canva, and many others.

Are there any refunds if I don't like the service?

Because we deliver custom design work it’s not possible to issue a refund.

Do you work at our company?

It may feel like it but we do not work at your company however we do stay in close touch to make sure we provide the best service possible.
